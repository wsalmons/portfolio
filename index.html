Wyatt Salmons

I am the founder of WSCWS, Athelion Photography, one of the founders of ABQatNight, native New Mexican, lover of New Mexican food, and dogs. I am a former EMT serving in Albuquerque and the surrounding area from 2015-2019, and also have experience in 3D modeling, and structural drafting. At WSCWS I aspire to create a simple, flexible platform to find solutions to web-based problems. I specialize in front end web-development, and design. I can often be found at local tech-related meet-ups, such as 1 Million Cups, and Techstars Startup Weekend. Feel free to browse through some of my previous projects, and dont forget to check out my blog, it journals my experiences of the ABQ tech ecosystem. Thank you for your time!
